CSWP — SOLIDWORKS CAD Design Professional

Verified credential: View my CSWP credential

Official certification page: solidworks.com/certifications/solidworks-cad-design-professional

What this certification covers (summary)

  • Create & modify fully-defined parametric parts per given drawings/specs.
  • Use design intent with sketches, constraints, equations, and global variables.
  • Advanced features: revolves, sweeps, lofts, shells, fillets/chamfers, patterns.
  • Configurations & design tables to handle design variants quickly.
  • Evaluate mass properties/center of mass; meet tolerance targets after changes.

CSWP-SM — SOLIDWORKS Sheet Metal Professional

Verified credential: View my CSWPA–SM credential

Official certification page: solidworks.com/certifications/cswpa-sheet-metal

What this certification covers (summary)

  • Sheet metal part creation from solid bodies or sketches; base flange & edge flange.
  • Bend features: sketched/hem/jog, miter & closed corners, break-corner/fillet.
  • Forming tools, louvers, beads; relief types and corner treatments.
  • Flatten, bend tables, K-factor / bend allowance basics, manufacturing readiness.
  • Editing bends, unfold/refold sequences; DXF export considerations.

CSWA-AM — SOLIDWORKS Additive Manufacturing Associate

Verified credential: View my CSWA-AM credential

Official certification page: solidworks.com/certifications/additive-manufacturing-cswa-additive-manufacturing

What this certification covers (summary)

  • AM fundamentals: processes (FDM, SLA, SLS, MJF, metal AM) and use-cases.
  • Design for Additive: overhangs, support strategy, wall/feature limits, tolerances.
  • Materials & properties: polymers vs. metals, anisotropy, post-processing basics.
  • Build prep: orientation, nesting, layer height, infill, shells; STL/3MF basics.
  • Quality & safety: defects, warping, validation checks, handling & lab safety.
