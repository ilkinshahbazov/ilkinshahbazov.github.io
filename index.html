CSWP — SOLIDWORKS CAD Design Professional

Verified credential: View my CSWP credential

Official certification page: solidworks.com/certifications/solidworks-cad-design-professional

What this certification covers (summary)

  • Create & modify fully-defined parametric parts per given drawings/specs.
  • Use design intent with sketches, constraints, equations, and global variables.
  • Advanced features: revolves, sweeps, lofts, shells, fillets/chamfers, patterns.
  • Configurations & design tables to handle design variants quickly.
  • Evaluate mass properties/center of mass; meet tolerance targets after changes.
